Privacy Policy

At Crafted Clipz, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

You have the right to:

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
